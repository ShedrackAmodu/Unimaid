About Ramat Library

Ramat Library is a modern library with a rich collection of print and electronic resources. We have 9 divisions, 13 branch Libraries, an American Space section and also an International Institute of Islamic thought section which provide access to information and knowledge to our users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality library services, promote information literacy and support the University's academic and research programs.
